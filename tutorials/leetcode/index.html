LeetCode Patterns — Overview & Study Plan

Interviews don't test 3000 problems — they test ~20 reusable patterns. Learn to recognize the pattern from the problem statement, apply its template, and adapt. That's the entire skill, and this track teaches it directly.

How Every Pattern Page Works

SectionWhat you get
ConceptWhat the pattern is and the intuition behind it
Recognition cuesExact phrases in problem statements that signal this pattern
Code templateA memorizable skeleton you adapt per problem
3 problems🟢 Easy → 🟡 Medium → 🔴 Hard, with hidden full solutions & complexity
Trade-offsWhen the pattern fails and what to reach for instead

Suggested Study Plan

Weeks 1–2: Foundations + Two Pointers, Sliding Window, Binary Search, Prefix Sum.
Weeks 3–4: Stack, Linked List, Intervals, Fast & Slow Pointers.
Weeks 5–6: Trees & Graphs (BFS/DFS, Topological Sort, Union-Find).
Weeks 7–8: Heaps, Backtracking, DP, Greedy, Trie, Bits — then mixed practice.

💡 Rule of thumb: solving 3 problems deeply (re-derive the solution from the template, explain complexity out loud) beats grinding 30 shallowly. That's why each pattern here has exactly 3 — chosen to cover the pattern's full difficulty range.
